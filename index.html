About the Host

Constantine Lentakis

There is an interesting story in everyone. This show attempts to get to the essence of the individual through long-form discussions about them as people and their fields of expertise.

If curiosity is at your core, give this a listen and together we can stumble through life unafraid of looking ignorant in the pursuit of becoming more informed.

Constantine brings a wide-ranging curiosity spanning psychology, philosophy, business, and culture to every conversation — creating a space where guests can open up about the experiences, ideas, and passions that define them.
